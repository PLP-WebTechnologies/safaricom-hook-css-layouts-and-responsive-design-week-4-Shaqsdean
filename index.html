Welcome to Gemili Collections

Gemili Collections is a boutique brand dedicated to offering unique, handcrafted pieces that tell a story. Founded by Gemma and Leo, the company started as a small, family-owned business with a passion for creating meaningful, one-of-a-kind items. From handwoven scarves to locally crafted jewelry, each piece in our collection is designed with care and crafted with love. At Gemili Collections, we believe in the beauty of authenticity and the power of craftsmanship. We work closely with artisans to bring you products that not only add character to your space or wardrobe but also support a sustainable and creative future.
